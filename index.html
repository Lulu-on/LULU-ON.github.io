Pink Cod

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cod 500 gr
Potatoes 700 gr
sugar beets about 4 beets
onions about 3 onions
olive oil 15 ml

Step 1: cook the sugar beets and the onions with olive oil for 10 minutes. Step 2: add the potatoes and cook for 15 minutes.

Orange Cheese

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Mozzarella Cheese 500 gr
Tomato 300 gr
Carrots about 5 carrots
garlic about 6 cloves
olive oil 15 ml

Step 1: cook the garlic, carrots and tomato all together with olive oil for 25 minutes. Step 2: add the cheese and cook just for 5 minutes.

Blue Tuna

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Tuna 500 gr
Mozzarella Cheese 200 gr
Tomato 300 gr
Black Olives about 100 gr
Garlic about 6 cloves
Olive Oil 15 ml

Step 1: cook the garlic, black olives and tomato all together with olive oil for 20 minutes. Step 2: add the Tuna and cook for 20 minutes. Step 3: add the cheese and wait just 5 minutes.
